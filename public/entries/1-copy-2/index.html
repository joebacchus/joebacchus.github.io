Exploring Fibonacci Numbers

The Fibonacci sequence is a famous integer sequence defined by the recurrence relation:

$$ F_n = F_{n-1} + F_{n-2}, \quad \text{with } F_0 = 0, ; F_1 = 1 $$

Golden Ratio Connection

As $ n \to \infty $, the ratio of consecutive Fibonacci numbers approaches the golden ratio:

$$ \varphi = \frac{1 + \sqrt{5}}{2} \approx 1.618 $$
